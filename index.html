Overview

The IROC (Imperial Rebels of [Classified]) was a Scratch Nation. It was created on 11/17/2024. It was later renamed to South Classified somewhere around January 2025.

        
History

The IROC was created on 11/17/2024 by @Operationrebirth, 17 days after the takeover of the [Classified] Union. It was a small Scratch Nation.
    It had 22 followers at it's max power. It was renamed to South Classified in January 2025 before being given to @IronLiam1. Little information 
    is known about this nation.
